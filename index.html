Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the questions we hear most often. Still have something on your mind? Just call us at 619-333-6470.

Do you provide emergency 24/7 support? expand_more
Yes! Electrical emergencies don't wait for business hours. We're on standby 24/7 for critical failures and safety hazards across all of Southern California.
How do I know if I need a panel upgrade? expand_more
Signs include frequently tripping breakers, flickering lights, a 100A or smaller panel, or plans to add an EV charger or major appliances. We assess your current panel and give you a straight answer with no upselling.
Do you pull permits, and why does that matter? expand_more
Yes, always. Permitted work is inspected for code compliance, protecting your safety, insurability, and resale value. We handle all permits and coordinate inspections.
Do you work on biotech and laboratory electrical systems? expand_more
Yes, it is one of our specialties. 12 years of experience with biotech labs, medical buildings, and research facilities. We handle dedicated instrument circuits, isolated power panels, emergency systems, and cleanroom wiring.
What areas do you serve? expand_more
Based in San Diego, serving all of San Diego County including East County and North County, Orange County, Los Angeles, San Bernardino, and Riverside. For large commercial projects we accommodate throughout SoCal.
Are your technicians licensed and insured? expand_more
Absolutely. SoCal Bradley Electric holds CA Contractor License #1131979, fully bonded and insured for residential and commercial work. Verify at cslb.ca.gov.
Are you the same as other Bradley Electric companies? expand_more
No. SoCal Bradley Electric is an independent, locally owned electrical company based in San Diego, CA. No affiliation with Bradley Electric companies in other states. CA License #1131979, verify at cslb.ca.gov.
